Delete your data

Applies to: Stretchy: Daily Mobility

Stretchy works without an account and stores your data on your own device, not on our servers. That means you are always in direct control of it.

Delete everything from within the App

  1. Open Stretchy.
  2. Go to the Profile tab.
  3. Under Data & privacy, tap Delete my data and confirm.

This permanently erases your plan, streak, progress, scores, and settings from the device. Uninstalling the App also removes this locally stored data.

What is deleted

  • Your setup preferences (focus areas, goal, experience, time).
  • Your plan, daily progress, streak, medals, and form-guidance scores.
  • Your coach choice and reminder settings.

Camera video is never stored or uploaded, so there is nothing to delete on that front — frames are analysed on-device and immediately discarded.

Data held about you on our servers

Stretchy does not currently hold any account or personal data on our servers. If this ever changes (for example, optional cloud sync or an account), this page will be updated, and you will be able to request deletion of that server-side data by emailing us. We will action verified deletion requests within 30 days.

Request help

If you have any trouble deleting your data, email help.stretchy@gmail.com and we will help.

Stretchy is operated by an independent developer based in Portugal.
