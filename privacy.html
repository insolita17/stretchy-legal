Privacy Policy

Effective date: 11 June 2026 · Version 2026-06

This Privacy Policy explains how the Stretchy: Daily Mobility mobile application (the "App") handles your information. Stretchy is operated by an independent developer based in Portugal ("we", "us", or "the operator"). By using the App you agree to the practices described below.

The short version: Stretchy is private by design. It works without an account, the camera runs entirely on your phone, and your plan and progress are stored locally on your device — not on our servers. We do not run ads, we do not track you across other apps, and we do not sell your data.

1. Information processed on your device

Stretchy keeps the following on your device, to make the App work. It is not transmitted to us:

  • Your preferences: the focus areas, goal, experience level, available time, and any areas to take it easy on that you enter during setup.
  • Your activity: your generated plan, daily progress, streak, medals, and form-guidance scores.
  • Your settings: chosen coach, reminder time, and whether reminders are on.

You can view and copy all of this at any time in Profile → Export my data, and erase it in Profile → Delete my data.

2. The camera and AI form guidance

Stretchy's optional camera guidance uses on-device computer vision (pose estimation) to read your body position and guide your stretching form.

  • Processing happens entirely on your phone. Camera frames are analysed in the moment and then discarded — they are never uploaded, never stored, and never sent to us or any third party.
  • It reads body position to guide form. It does not identify who you are and does not perform facial recognition. Because we do not use it to uniquely identify anyone, this is not "special category" biometric data under the GDPR.
  • AI transparency (EU AI Act): the camera is an AI / computer-vision system that provides general movement guidance. It is not a medical assessment and it can make mistakes.
  • The camera is opt-in: it only runs when you choose to start a guided scan, and only after your device grants camera permission.

3. Wellness, not medical

Stretchy provides general wellness and mobility guidance. It does not diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any condition, and is not a substitute for professional medical advice. If a movement causes sharp pain or numbness, or you are recovering from injury or surgery, please consult a qualified professional and stop anything that hurts.

4. Subscriptions

Applies only if and when you buy a Stretchy subscription. Purchases are processed by Apple App Store or Google Play under their own privacy policies; we receive only the subscription status needed to unlock premium features (we never receive your full payment details). If we later use a subscription-management provider (such as RevenueCat) or other processors, this policy will be updated to name them before they go live.

5. Notifications

If you turn on the daily reminder, it is scheduled locally on your device. We do not operate a server that sends you notifications, and enabling reminders does not share any data with us.

6. What we do not do

  • We do not require an account to use the App.
  • We do not run third-party advertising.
  • We do not use cross-app or cross-site tracking.
  • We do not sell or rent your personal data.
  • We do not upload your camera frames or use the camera to identify you.

7. Legal bases (GDPR)

  • Your consent — for using the camera for form guidance (you can withdraw it any time by not using the camera or revoking the permission in your device settings).
  • Performance of our agreement with you — to provide the core App features you request.

8. Retention

Your data stays on your device until you delete it in the App or uninstall the App. Uninstalling removes the locally stored data.

9. Your rights

Under the GDPR you have the right to access, export (portability), correct, and erase your personal data, and to object to or restrict processing. Because Stretchy stores your data on your own device, you can exercise these directly: Export my data and Delete my data in Profile. You may also contact us (below), and you have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data-protection supervisory authority.

10. Children

Stretchy is intended for users aged 18 and over. It is not directed to children, and we do not knowingly process children's data.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the App evolves (for example, when premium subscriptions or optional cloud sync are introduced). We will post the updated version here with a new effective date.

12. Contact

Questions or requests: help.stretchy@gmail.com.

Stretchy is operated by an independent developer based in Portugal. This document is provided for transparency and does not constitute legal advice.
