Common questions

  • Does the camera record me? No. Camera form guidance runs entirely on your phone; frames are analysed in the moment and discarded — never stored or uploaded.
  • Do I need an account? No. Stretchy works as a guest; your data stays on your device.
  • How do I delete my data? Profile → Data & privacy → Delete my data. See Delete your data.
  • How do I export my data? Profile → Export my data copies everything stored on your device.
  • Is Stretchy medical advice? No — it offers general wellness and mobility guidance. Stop any movement that hurts and consult a professional if you have a health concern.

Stretchy is operated by an independent developer based in Portugal.
